II. HOW TO JOIN

  1. A Verified Member may join the Promotion by going to the MoveFWD section of the Website and signing up for any of the open activities appearing in the section ("Activity" or "Activities") by clicking the "Sign Me Up" button under an Activity. A Verified Member who joins the Promotion is referred to as a "Participant".
  2. A Participant should ensure that he is available on the date or period specified for the Activity before signing up.
  3. A Participant may sign up for more than one Activity at any given time.
  4. Sign-up for an Activity shall remain open only for a predetermined length of time (a "Cycle") which, owing to the nature of the Promotion, shall not be disclosed to the Participants. After a Cycle, sign-ups for current Activities shall be closed and sign-ups for new Activities may be opened.
  5. Upon signing up for an Activity, the Participant shall be given the option to either "Play" or "Pledge"
    • By choosing to "Play", the Participant needs to successfully accomplish a certain number of undisclosed tasks for a chance to win a slot in the Activity.
    • By choosing to "pledge", the Participant shall give up the specified number of points for a chance to win a slot in the Activity. The points shall be permanently deducted from the Participant's points register even if the Participant is not awarded a slot in the Activity.

    Please note that PMFTC reserves the right to remove the "Pledge" option for certain Activities.

  6. A Participant who chooses to "Play" may change his option and choose to "Pledge" at any time before he accomplishes and submits his first task. Once he makes a submission under "Play", the Participant may no longer change his option to "Pledge".A Participant who chooses to "Pledge" may no longer change his option to "Play".
  7. Simply completing the tasks under "Play" or giving up the points under "Pledge" does not guarantee the Participant a slot in the Activity as time is of the essence in this Promotion. Participants shall be evaluatedat the end of a Cycle according to the order that they successfully completed the tasks (if they chose to "Play") or that they gave up their points (if they chose to "Pledge"). "Play" Participants and "Pledge" Participants shall be evaluated together.
  8. Evaluation shall be in accordance with the rules stated in these Mechanics.

III. HOW TO "PLAY"

  1. Participants shall be required to submit proof of completion of the task(s) through any of the following ways ("Submissions"):
    • Uploading a photo onto the specific Activity section
    • Uploading a video onto the specific Activity section
    • Posting a comment on a specified section of the Website
    • Entering a code provided by PMFTC or its authorized representatives on the specific Activity section
  2. Uploaded content or posted comments are subject to moderation and the approval of PMFTC or its authorized representatives prior to being accepted or published on the Website. Entered codes are subject to verification by PMFTC or its authorized representatives.
  3. A Task shall only be considered completed upon approval or verification by PMFTC of the Submission. Approval or verification shall be made within twenty-four (24) hours from the Submission.
  4. The Participant shall be allowed to move on to the next task once his Submission is approved or verified. The number of tasks that a Participant must accomplish may differ for each Activity.
  5. "Play" is considered completed when the Submission for the last task is verified or approved.
  6. Upon completion, the Participant will be advised to wait for the announcement on who will be awarded slots to the Activity, which shall be made at the end of the Cycle.

IV. HOW TO "PLEDGE"

  1. The "Pledge" value varies perActivity.A Participant who decides to "Pledge" must have the requisite number of points available in his points register. If not, he must first try to earn points or choose to "Play" instead.
  2. "Pledge" is considered completed when the points have been deducted from the Participant's points register.
  3. Upon completion, the Participant will be advised to wait for the announcement on who will be awarded slots to the Activity, which shall be made at the end of the Cycle.
